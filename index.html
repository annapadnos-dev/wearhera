About HERA

HERA was created to fill a gap in the market: activewear that is both safe and stylish. Inspired by the Greek goddess and protector of women, HERA exists to give girls and young women a better option for the clothes they wear closest to their skin.

Our Mission

We believe girls should not have to choose between health and style. HERA is committed to empowering young women through safe materials, thoughtful design, and a mission that supports confidence, strength, and self-expression.
