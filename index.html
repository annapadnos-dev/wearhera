About HERA

HERA was created to fill a gap in the market: activewear that is both safe and stylish. Inspired by the Greek goddess and protector of women, HERA exists to give girls and young women a better option for the clothes they wear closest to their skin.

Our Mission

Health + Style

We believe girls should not have to choose between looking good and wearing materials they feel good about.

Confidence + Empowerment

HERA is built to support confidence, strength, and self-expression through thoughtful design and a mission-driven brand.
